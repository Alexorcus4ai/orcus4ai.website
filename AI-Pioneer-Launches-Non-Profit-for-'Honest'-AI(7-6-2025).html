AI Pioneer Launches Non-Profit for 'Honest' AI


By Abin/Alex cofounder of orcus4ai | News | 7 june 2025

Yoshua Bengio, a leading figure in artificial intelligence and well-known for his work in deep learning, has launched LawZero—a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting transparency and ethical standards in AI systems. LawZero's primary objective is to develop frameworks and tools to prevent AI agents from deceiving users, addressing critical risks like misinformation, manipulation, and the emergence of uncontrolled autonomous systems.

Bengio, who will serve as president, brings extensive technical expertise to the initiative. The organization's mission is particularly relevant as the deployment of advanced AI systems accelerates in a landscape already marked by a multi-trillion dollar technological arms race. LawZero's efforts are expected to shape industry practices, influence regulatory policy, and support the development of AI technologies that are robust, trustworthy, and aligned with human values.

Ensuring ethical AI development is increasingly vital as these systems become embedded in daily life, and LawZero represents a significant step toward addressing growing concerns over the societal impacts of artificial intelligence.
