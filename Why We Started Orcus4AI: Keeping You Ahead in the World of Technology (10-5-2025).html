Why We Started Orcus4AI: Keeping You Ahead in the World of Technology

By Abin/Alex cofounder of orcus4ai | Introduction | 10 May 2025

Let’s be real-keeping up with AI and tech these days feels like trying to drink from a firehose. New tools, research papers, apps, and breakthroughs pop up almost every hour. As students, developers, entrepreneurs, or just curious minds, it can get overwhelming fast. We felt that too.

That’s exactly why we built Orcus4AI.

We were tired of spending hours digging through endless articles, social media threads, and outdated blogs just to stay in the loop. So, we thought: What if there was one place that did all the heavy lifting for us? A smart companion that keeps track of everything happening in AI and tech-and delivers it straight to you, in real time.

That’s the heart of Orcus4AI.

We built it for people like us: the curious, the busy, the always-learning. Whether you're a student trying to keep up with the latest tools, a developer hunting for new frameworks, or an enthusiast who just wants to know what’s going on, Orcus4AI is here to make it effortless.

It updates constantly-daily, hourly, sometimes by the minute—so you never miss what matters. No fluff, no noise, just reliable, up-to-date info.

At the end of the day, we started Orcus4AI because we needed it ourselves. And now, we’re excited to share it with you.

Let’s stay ahead of the curve-together.
