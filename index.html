What is ThinkBig.blog?
ThinkBig is a passion project — a place to explore the ideas in tech, science, and innovation that genuinely fascinate us. No sponsored content, no clickbait. Just clear, honest writing for people who want to actually understand what's happening in the world.
Who writes the articles?
Everything is written by a single author with a background in engineering and a deep obsession with how things work. Occasionally, guest contributors from different fields share their perspective on something exciting in their domain.
How often do you publish?
Roughly once a week. The goal is quality over quantity — every piece goes through several drafts before it's published. Subscribe to the newsletter to get each new article delivered to your inbox.
Is ThinkBig free to read?
Yes, completely free. No paywalls, no ads, no tracking. This is a passion project and we want as many curious people reading it as possible. If you enjoy the writing, the best thing you can do is share it with someone who'd appreciate it.
